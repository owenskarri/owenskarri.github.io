Emerging talented multimedia designer enrolled at Eastern Washington University-Visual Communication Design program as a Post-Bachelor. I received my first Bachelors at Washington State University in Apparel Merchandising & Textiles, minoring in Business Administration. The past 15 years I have showed my entrepreneurial spirit by starting and managing my own businesses, Owens Quarter Horses and DesignsbyKarKar.

Owens Quarter Horses was in business for 10 years, where I bought and sold show horses on the AQHA Circuit. As business owner and competitor I have acquired a tremendous understanding of the Pleasure Horse Industry and parlaying those talents into an emerging equine marketing freelance business.

In 2010 I started DesignsbyKarKar, custom hat & applique business tailored to the equine industry. My hats have been sold all over the world and are as unique as they come.

I enjoy collaborating with clients in achieving their desired goal in my design work. Services I provide are graphic design for print or web, video editing, web sites and photography. I am your one stop shop for Multimedia Design. My skills will deepen and advance as I progress through school. I am schedule to have my Bachelors of Arts in Visual Design 2017.
